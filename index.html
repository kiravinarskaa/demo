Train People to Recognize Phishing Before It Causes Damage

CyberSafe Trainer is an interactive awareness platform that helps students and beginners practice recognizing phishing emails, suspicious links, and social engineering tricks.

Start Training

Why this project matters

Phishing attacks continue to target universities, companies, and ordinary users. Many victims are not hacked because of weak technology, but because they trust fake messages that look real.

This project focuses on the human factor in cybersecurity and provides a simple, visual, and practical way to improve awareness.

Main Features

Interactive Scenarios

Users read realistic phishing messages and decide what action to take.

Instant Feedback

The platform explains why a message is suspicious and helps users learn from mistakes.

Simple Learning Experience

The interface is designed for beginners, students, and awareness training demos.

How it works

1. Read

Open a scenario and inspect the sender, message, and link.

2. Decide

Choose the action you think is safest.

3. Learn

Get feedback, improve your score, and understand phishing clues.
