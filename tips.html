Cybersecurity Awareness Tips

Use these simple rules to detect suspicious messages and stay safer online.

Check the Sender

Attackers often use addresses that look official but contain small changes.

Do Not Trust Urgency

Messages that pressure you to act immediately are often suspicious.

Inspect Every Link

A strange or unofficial-looking domain is one of the strongest warning signs.

Never Share Passwords

Real institutions should not ask for passwords through links in emails.

Verify Through Official Channels

If a message looks suspicious, contact the organization directly.

Pause Before Acting

A few seconds of checking can prevent data loss and account compromise.

Quick checklist

  • Is the sender address real?
  • Is the message creating fear or urgency?
  • Does the link look suspicious?
  • Are you asked for sensitive information?
  • Can you verify the request in another way?
